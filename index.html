Bioinformatics Hub Of Kenya Initiative

BHKi bridges the gap between well established bioinformaticians
and aspiring ones through peer training and mentorship
so as to enhance collaborations to foster quality
scientific research and innovations and
promote the sharing of data,
skills and codes.

Our Vision

To be a vivacious, well-coordinated and enthusiastic hub that will develop bioinformatics and skills
set through creating awareness, collaborations and capacity development.

Our Mission

Our goal is to create a community that links all bioinformaticians, early career to expert, to make
knowledge transfer faster and therefore making research faster in our region.

~Research can no longer be done in silos and community allows its propagation.

The scope for Bioinformatics is wide in the region

Research Prospects

Africa presents a large playground for scientific research given its diversity in people and cultures.

Community

No more silos

Our Approach

To sensitize researchers about bioinformatics, train them on necessary skills,
create platforms for them to build their skills, build a community where they can interact, and collaborate.

Sensitize about Bioinformatics

Build & Train on Necessary Skills

Collaborative Community

Our Solution

Our activities are centered around our approach. We conduct outreaches, seminars, workshops, hackathons,
and conferences. Check out our activities page.

Our Impact

University outreaches reaching over 400 students, training students across multiple countries, and a community with over 300 members.
