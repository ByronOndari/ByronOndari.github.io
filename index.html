Bioinformatics Hub of Kenya Initiative

BHKi bridges the gap between well established bioinformaticians and aspiring ones through peer training
and mentorship so as to enhance collaborations to foster quality scientific research and
innovations and promote the sharing of data, skills and codes.

Our Vision

To be a vivacious, well-coordinated and enthusiastic hub that develops bioinformatics and skillsets through creating awareness, fostering collaborations and capacity development.

Our Mission

To build a community that connects bioinformaticians of all levels, early career to experts, enabling faster knowledge transfer and accelerating research in our region.

The bioinformatics landscape in our region

> 200 Universities

Over 200 universities in East Africa provide research training in life sciences and computer science at the undergraduate level.

1 Community: Breaking Down Silos

Commununity drives research by encouraging collaboration, knowledge sharing eventually resulting in better and faster research.

< 5% research output

Low-income countries contribute less than 5% of the world's scientific publications, highlighting the need for increased research output and knowledge dissemination.

Our Approach

We believe in raising awareness about bioinformatics, providing essential skills training,creating platforms
for skill development, fostering an interactive community, and facilitating collaborations.

Building and Training Essential Skills

We empower scientists by providing inclusive and accessible training programs, ensuring that knowledge in bioinformatics is accessible to all, regardless of background or expertise.

Raising Awareness about Bioinformatics

We empower scientists with the knowledge and resources they need to navigate the exciting realm of bioinformatics.

Fostering a Collaborative Community

With our inclusive approach, we create a supportive environment that fosters the knowledge exchange, empowering scientists to collaborate and drive innovative breakthroughs.

Our Solution

Our activities are the heart and soul of our solution, encompassing a range of captivating events.

Ignite curiosity and foster connections through outreaches. We bring bioinformatics to students and researchers, inspiring them to explore the fascinating world of computational biology and data-driven discovery.

Outreaches

Unleash creativity and problem-solving through workshops and hackathons. We foster an environment of hands-on learning and collaboration, providing participants with the essential skills and tools to tackle real-world challenges in bioinformatics.

Workshops & Hackathons

Networking and knowledge sharing through conferences. Our seminars and conferences are a world of knowledge and inspiration as experts from diverse fields come together to share groundbreaking research and insights, fostering collaboration and pushing the boundaries of innovation
