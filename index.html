Join us as we bring you exclusive interviews with experts in their fields and hear firsthand accounts of their journey through a STEM career. From the struggles to the successes, we explore the challenges faced by professionals in STEM fields, while uncovering insights and advice on how to thrive in these ever-evolving industries. Our mission is to inspire and inform the next generation of STEM leaders while providing valuable insights for those already established in their careers. We hope you enjoy the episodes. This is the Science in Real Life!

The Science in Real Life podcast (SIRL) is more than just a podcast; it's a community of curious minds, STEM enthusiasts, and lifelong leraners.
