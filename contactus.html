Contact Us

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join BHKi?

That’s easy. You can fill out the form on the JOIN US page, pay a small fee of KES 500 or $5, send us an email with the payment details, and you’ll get an email saying you are officially a member.

Do you offer training and what type of training?

Yes we do offer training. Introduction to Bioinformatics and Open science, programming in Python and R, -omics training such as metagenomics. We also design training material and get instructors so you can request for training in specific areas. Send us an email at info@bhki.org. Or fill out the contact us form with the inquiry subject: TRAINING

How do I get mentorship?

You can fill out the contact us form with the inquiry subject: MENTORSHIP writing specific details about what you need specifically, and we will get back to you with someone best suited to answer your questions.

Do you offer internships or attachments?

Unfortunately we do not offer internships or attachments, but we post opportunities forwarded to us by various organizations on open positions. Always check our website, or your email if you are a member for such details.
